About

Breast cancer is a deadly non-skin disease that most usually affects women. It is generated for a variety of reasons, ranging from cells and glandular ducts to the breast's supporting tissues, with the exception of the skin, There are two types of breast cancer Benign (B) and Malign (M).

Benign

Benign cancer or can be called a tumor, which has the property of not damaging the surrounding cells. This benign cancer can heal by itself. However, in some cases still require medical treatment.

Malign

Malignant tumors or can be called cancer, are spread and can damage the cells that are around them. As we know that, cancer is a disease caused by the abnormal growth of normal body tissue cells. Malignant cancer grows rapidly and can cause death.
